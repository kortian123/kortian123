Explore Source of the Nile

Jinja is located in the east of Kampala and it happens to be one of the oldest industrialized towns in Uganda. Its where most of the Indians settled before Amin’s regime which saw them deported and they did a lot of intermarriages there. When you walk through the streets of Jinja you can still those ancient Indian buildings, it basically has not changed that much. The source of the Nile happens to be in Jinja, this has seen many people flog the place to experience and see the very origin of one of the longest rivers in the world. River Nile originates in Uganda and ends up in Egypt crossing about 5 African countries.
